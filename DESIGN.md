# The Carburetor Whisperer — design tokens (Mechanical Archive)

A dark, oil-stained machinist's workbench. Industrial brutalism meets tactile
skeuomorphism: deep charcoal surfaces under a single overhead shop light,
aged-brass highlights, stamped Anton headlines, carbon-copy typewriter body, and
condensed Archivo Narrow labels.

Signature motifs: the brass **parts-tag** (clipped corner + punched hole),
tactile **stamped buttons** (hard offset shadow that presses down on click),
**milled-slot** inputs (recessed, inset shadow), **scratched-metal** hairline
dividers, and a fixed metallic **grain** overlay across the whole surface.
Sharp 0px corners throughout; depth comes from ambient shadow and containment,
not rounding.
